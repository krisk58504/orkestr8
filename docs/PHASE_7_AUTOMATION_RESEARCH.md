# Phase 7 Automation — Competitor Analysis & Market Gap Thesis

I reviewed what major competitors currently automate and where the biggest gaps still exist. Below is the guidance for Orkestr8.

We should absolutely build automation as a core differentiator.

## Competitor Automation Review

### Buildium

Automates well:
- recurring rent charges
- autopay
- rent reminders
- late fees
- payment receipts
- listing syndication
- tenant applications
- tenant screening workflows
- maintenance request intake
- assign work orders
- recurring maintenance reminders
- recurring accounting workflows

AI:
- AI writing assistance
- AI bill scanning
- light workflow assistance

Weaknesses:
- automation builder is limited
- vendor workflows are shallow
- communications are fragmented
- AI is mostly assistive, not workflow-native
- limited cross-module workflow automation

### AppFolio

Strongest automation among current competitors.

Automates:
- leasing inquiry routing
- guest cards
- follow-up sequences
- tour scheduling
- resident onboarding
- rent collection
- work order routing
- inspections
- vendor notifications
- invoice workflows
- reporting workflows

AI:
- leasing conversational AI
- workflow assistance
- guided automation

Weaknesses:
- vendor management depth still limited
- automation customization can feel constrained
- no truly unified inbox
- dispatch workflows not as deep as field-service style systems

### RentRedi

AI:

## Biggest Gaps in Market

These are the strongest opportunities for Orkestr8.

### 1. True workflow automation builder

Build a flexible:

Trigger → Conditions → Actions

Examples:

Maintenance request submitted

Conditions:
- category = plumbing
- priority = emergency
- after_hours = true

Actions:
- notify on-call vendor
- notify PM
- open emergency thread
- start SLA timer
- AI draft resident reply

Very few PMS systems handle this well.

### 2. Vendor automation (major opportunity)

Examples:

Vendor insurance expires in 15 days

Actions:
- email vendor
- notify PM
- add warning badge

Insurance expires

Actions:
- block assignments
- escalate

Vendor declines job

Actions:
- route to backup vendor
- notify PM
- update ETA

Vendor invoice uploaded

Actions:
- notify accounting
- flag invoice if over approved amount

This is a major differentiator.

### 3. Unified communication automation

Examples:

Tenant emails:
"Water leaking under sink"

System:
- detect maintenance issue
- identify unit
- create work order
- AI categorize
- AI draft response
- notify PM

Lead inquiry received

System:
- create lead
- assign leasing
- send AI follow-up

Vendor messages:
"Cannot make appointment"

System:
- update work order
- notify PM
- suggest alternate vendor

This is a major opportunity and should feel seamless.

### 4. AI operational insights

Examples:

Unit 304:
3 plumbing tickets in 45 days

AI:
Recommend preventative inspection

Vendor:
Missed SLA 3 times

AI:
Flag performance risk

Portfolio:
High vacancy risk

AI:
Recommend leasing campaign

These insights should feel built-in and actionable.
